01 / THE HANDOFF

A procedure built for the next person.

The job is not to make rough notes sound polished. It is to remove the assumptions that stop someone else from running the process correctly.

01

A clear start and finish

The document names the trigger, operator, prerequisites, expected result, and evidence that the work is complete.

02

Actions someone can test

Conversation and commentary become numbered steps with owners, decisions, and completion criteria.

03

The non-happy path

Quality gates, stop conditions, and escalation routes are pulled into the procedure instead of left in someone's memory.

02 / WORK SAMPLE

Read the output before you make contact.

03 / PROJECT BOUNDARY

One workflow. Safe source material. Defined finish.

The starter engagement is deliberately narrow: one repeatable workflow, one intended operator, and one clear outcome.

You provide

  • Notes, transcript, or current checklist
  • Redacted screenshots when needed
  • The intended operator and end state
  • One contact for workflow questions

Mackanic delivers

  • Controlled SOP in an editable format
  • Separate field checklist
  • Cold-read quality review
  • One consolidated revision round
